# Privacy Policy for Birje

_Last updated: [DATE]_

Thank you for playing **Birje**. Your privacy matters, and this game is built to respect it. This policy explains what information the app does — and does not — handle.

## The short version
**Birje does not collect, store, or share any personal information.** There are no accounts, no sign-in, and no servers. The game works fully offline.

## Information we collect
**None.** Birje does not collect personal data such as your name, email address, location, contacts, photos, or device identifiers. We do not use analytics or tracking technologies.

## Data stored on your device
Your game progress — high score, unlocked cosmetic skins, Sparkle Shards, settings, and daily-reward status — is saved **locally on your device only**, using the operating system's standard local storage. This data never leaves your device, is not transmitted to us or anyone else, and is removed if you uninstall the app or clear its data.

## Advertising
Birje does **not** display ads and does **not** include advertising or tracking SDKs.

## In-app purchases
Birje does **not** offer in-app purchases. All in-game currency (Sparkle Shards) is earned by playing.

## Children's privacy
Birje is family-friendly and suitable for all ages. Because the app collects no personal information from anyone, it collects no personal information from children.

## Third-party services
Birje does not integrate third-party services that collect data.

## Changes to this policy
If the app changes in a way that affects this policy (for example, if optional features are added in a future version), this policy will be updated and the "Last updated" date above will change. Any data-handling change will also be reflected in the app stores' privacy declarations.
